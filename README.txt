Software package for the calculation of the friction and
diffusion tensors of flexible molecules in the liquid phase.

Flexibility can be given in terms of Z-Matrix like internal
coordinates or as linear combinations of such coordinates.

To intall and use, please refer to the guide in the doc/ folder.

If results obtained with DiTe2 are published, please cite:

Campeggio, J.; Polimeno, A.; Zerbetto, M. DiTe2: Calculating the Diffusion
Tensor for Flexible Molecules. J. Comput. Chem. 2019, 40, 697-705.
